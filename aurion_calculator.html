⬡ Legal notice & model disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. This calculator derives all output values from a deterministic mathematical model based on the on-chain parameters of the AURION Meteora DLMM pool: wall₀ = 1,000 USDC, factor = 1.003, bin_step = 0.15%. It does not constitute financial advice, investment advice, a solicitation, or an offer to buy or sell any asset or security in any jurisdiction.

Buy-side model. The investment is simulated as sequential upward traversal of liquidity bins beginning at the entry price bin. Each bin b contains tokens(b) = 1,000 × (1.003/1.0015)^b AURI at price(b) = 1.0015^b USDC. Average buy price (VWAP) = total USDC spent ÷ total AURI acquired. Bins traversed upward = the number of bins the price advances due solely to this purchase.

Sell-side model. Selling is simulated as sequential downward traversal beginning at the target exit bin. Each bin b absorbs tokens(b) AURI and returns wall(b) = 1,000 × 1.003^b USDC. Average sell price (VWAP) = total USDC received ÷ total AURI sold. Bins pushed down = the number of bins the price retreats due to this sell. This model assumes sufficient USDC liquidity exists at the exit price level as a result of prior purchases by other market participants.

Fees excluded. Pool fee (0.03%) and Solana network fees are not modelled. Actual proceeds will be lower than shown. Results may differ materially from model projections due to real-time market conditions, concurrent activity, and available bin liquidity. AURI is not registered as a security in any jurisdiction. The AURION project does not guarantee any return. Verify all parameters independently on Solana Mainnet before making any decision.
