⬡ Legal notice & model disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. This calculator derives all output values from a deterministic mathematical model based on the on-chain parameters of the AURION Meteora DLMM pool: wall₀ = 1,000 USDC, factor = 1.003, bin_step = 0.15%, pool fee = 0.03%. It does not constitute financial advice, investment advice, a solicitation, or an offer to buy or sell any asset or security in any jurisdiction.

Buy-side model. The investment is simulated as sequential upward traversal of liquidity bins, beginning at the nearest bin below the entered price. Each bin b provides a fixed quantity of AURI — tokens(b) = 1,000 × (1.003/1.0015)^b — at price(b) = 1.0015^b USDC. A 0.03% pool fee is deducted from AURI received. Partial bin fills are supported proportionally.

Sell-side model. Selling is simulated as sequential downward traversal of bins, beginning at the nearest bin below the target exit price. Each bin absorbs tokens(b) AURI and returns wall(b) = 1,000 × 1.003^b USDC. The resulting price depression — the sell impact — reflects the number of bins the price retreats through before the full position is liquidated. A 0.03% pool fee is deducted from USDC received. This model assumes that (i) sufficient USDC liquidity has accumulated in the pool from prior purchases by other market participants, and (ii) no concurrent market activity affects execution.

Important limitations. Actual execution depends on real-time pool state, concurrent market activity, available bin liquidity, and network conditions at the moment of the transaction. Results may differ materially from model projections. AURI is not registered as a security in any jurisdiction. The AURION project does not guarantee any return on investment. Verify all on-chain parameters independently before making any decision.
