Architectural guarantees
⬡
Mint authority permanently revoked
Total supply is fixed at 1,000,000,000 AURI. Mint Authority is set to null — irreversibly. No one, including the founder, can ever create additional tokens. Verify on Solscan ↗
◇
Zero team allocation
No vesting, no insider addresses, no pre-sale. The founder holds zero tokens — this is architecturally enforced, not a promise. The entire supply is deployed as pool liquidity. The team earns only from the 0.03% swap fee.
◈
USDC withdrawal is impossible
The on-chain program contains no instruction for withdrawing buyer USDC from the pool. This is not a lock — the capability simply does not exist in the code. The program binary is byte-verified on-chain.
△
S-curve liquidity model
The USDC wall grows +0.3% per bin while price grows only +0.15%. Each successive price level requires more capital to reach. Price accelerates initially, then decelerates — an S-curve governed by math, not market psychology.
S-curve model
Three constants define everything
The entire AURION economy derives from one formula with three on-chain constants. No phases, no conditions, no switches.
price(b) = 1.0015b   |   wall(b) = 1000 × 1.003b   |   tokens(b) = 1000 × 1.001498b
bin_step = 0.15%  ·  wall₀ = 1,000 USDC  ·  factor = 1.003  ·  eff = factor/price_step ≈ 1.001498
Because factor (1.003) exceeds price_step (1.0015), the USDC depth of each bin grows faster than the price. Clearing bin 0 costs 1,000 USDC. Clearing bin 2,000 costs 399,821 USDC. This is a mathematical property of the protocol — not a market hypothesis.
